States of India

Andhra Pradesh

Known for its rich culture, history, and scenic beauty. Key attractions include Tirupati, Araku Valley, and Visakhapatnam.

Arunachal Pradesh

Known as the "Land of the Rising Sun", it is famous for its monasteries, beautiful landscapes, and unique tribal culture.

Assam

Renowned for its tea gardens, wildlife sanctuaries, and the mighty Brahmaputra River.

Bihar

Known for its ancient history and cultural heritage. Key attractions include Bodh Gaya, Nalanda University, and Patna.

Chhattisgarh

Famous for its tribal culture, natural beauty, and historical sites like Chitrakote Falls and Bastar.

Goa

Known for its stunning beaches, vibrant nightlife, and Portuguese-influenced architecture.

Gujarat

Famous for its rich cultural heritage, historical sites, and diverse wildlife. Key attractions include the Rann of Kutch and Gir National Park.

Haryana

Known for its rich history, vibrant culture, and contributions to the agricultural sector.

Himachal Pradesh

Famous for its scenic beauty, hill stations, and adventure sports. Key attractions include Shimla, Manali, and Dharamshala.

Jharkhand

Rich in mineral resources, forests, and wildlife. Key attractions include Ranchi, Jamshedpur, and Netarhat.

Karnataka

Known for its diverse culture, historical monuments, and natural beauty. Key attractions include Bangalore, Mysore, and Hampi.

Kerala

Known as "God's Own Country", famous for its backwaters, beaches, and hill stations.

Madhya Pradesh

Known for its rich history, cultural heritage, and wildlife. Key attractions include Khajuraho, Bhopal, and Bandhavgarh National Park.

Maharashtra

Famous for its diverse culture, historical sites, and vibrant cities. Key attractions include Mumbai, Pune, and Ajanta-Ellora Caves.

Manipur

Known for its scenic beauty, rich culture, and unique traditions. Key attractions include Imphal, Loktak Lake, and Keibul Lamjao National Park.

Meghalaya

Known for its stunning landscapes, waterfalls, and living root bridges. Key attractions include Shillong, Cherrapunji, and Mawlynnong.

Mizoram

Famous for its scenic beauty, vibrant culture, and pleasant climate. Key attractions include Aizawl, Lunglei, and Champhai.

Nagaland

Known for its unique culture, festivals, and scenic landscapes. Key attractions include Kohima, Dimapur, and Mokokchung.

Odisha

Famous for its rich cultural heritage, ancient temples, and beautiful beaches. Key attractions include Bhubaneswar, Puri, and Konark.

Punjab

Known for its vibrant culture, historical sites, and delicious cuisine. Key attractions include Amritsar, Chandigarh, and Ludhiana.

Rajasthan

Known for its royal heritage, magnificent forts, and palaces. Key attractions include Jaipur, Udaipur, and Jaisalmer.

Sikkim

Famous for its stunning landscapes, Buddhist monasteries, and adventure sports. Key attractions include Gangtok, Pelling, and Yumthang Valley.

Tamil Nadu

Known for its rich culture, ancient temples, and beautiful beaches. Key attractions include Chennai, Madurai, and Ooty.

Telangana

Known for its historical sites, vibrant culture, and IT hub. Key attractions include Hyderabad, Warangal, and Nizamabad.

Tripura

Known for its rich cultural heritage, palaces, and wildlife sanctuaries. Key attractions include Agartala, Ujjayanta Palace, and Neermahal.

Uttar Pradesh

Famous for its historical and cultural significance. Key attractions include Varanasi, Agra, and Lucknow.

Uttarakhand

Known for its scenic beauty, hill stations, and adventure sports. Key attractions include Dehradun, Nainital, and Rishikesh.

West Bengal

Known for its rich cultural heritage, historical sites, and natural beauty. Key attractions include Kolkata, Darjeeling, and Sundarbans.
